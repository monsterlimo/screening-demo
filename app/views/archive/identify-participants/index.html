User testing (1)

  • Wombwell GP - 29th Nov - Practice Manager / Nurse / Admin - 90mins

Key Findings (3)

  • History prototype, clear the sample taker doesn't need to see as much as CSAS, the nurse only uses the history on OE in special circumstances (such as the patient moving to the practice.) Mainly just logs in for the HMR101 (which they mentioned would be good digitised) and if they do want to see the history they only wants a brief snapshot of the last test and patient demographics.
  • At the moment the results history on OE is coded e.g 2 means negative they would rather it just said negative so they didn’t have to rely on remembering.
  • They weren’t keen on having the journey at the top of the page but said it might be useful sometimes so maybe under a tab.

Design Challenges (2)

  • How do we make it clears what the codes means
  • How do we make it so that the information is clearer that is most relevant
